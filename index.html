This website contains a range of spatial design information regarding
the Bridge of Remembrance and Victoria Square. This involves a range of
researched information as well as photos of each site. Each button will
take you to the corresponding page of each site, and has a navigation bar
to take you to subcategories of each said page.

Conclusion


Both The Bridge of Remembrance and Victoria Square have significant cultural
value to not only Christchurch but the very land that they stand on. Both
sites also have designs that reflect this cultural significance of both
pakeha and MƒÅori history, bringing these 2 cultures together in the same way
these places bring people together. They also have significance to the city
of Christchurch, both sites are located in the CBD and seemingly have the
entire city built around them. These sites marking the centre of such a
bustling city shows that we build our civilisation and society around how
we are able to bring our cultures together in such a way as we do with
Victoria Square and the Bridge of Remembrance.
